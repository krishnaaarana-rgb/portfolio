The Deployment Protocol

How we go from "Audit" to "Autopilot" in 14 days.

01. The Audit

We don't guess. We track. I install monitoring on your current workflows to identify the highest-leverage bottlenecks based on data, not intuition.

02. The Architecture

Designing the brain. Selection of LLMs (Claude vs GPT-4), database structure, and logic flows. You get a full blueprint before a line of code is written.

03. Injection & Testing

We deploy agents in "Shadow Mode" first. They observe and draft responses for human approval. Once accuracy hits 99%, we remove the human.

04. Handover

You own the code. I provide full documentation and training videos so your team can manage the system (or keep me on retainer to do it).
